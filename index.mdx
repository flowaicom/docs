## Why use Flow AI?

Flow AI accelerates AI agent development with **validated test data** that your teams can trust.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create robust test data faster">
    Turn weeks of manual curation into hours with AI-generated test cases grounded in your real-world data and production patterns.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Expert validation built-in">
    Your domain experts validate and refine test cases through an intuitive UI, ensuring datasets reflect real-world requirements and edge cases.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Evolve with your agents">
    As your AI agents improve and new patterns emerge, Flow AI keeps your test datasets relevant and comprehensive.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
